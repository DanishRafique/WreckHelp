Company History

Our story doesn’t get told without you. We’ve come a long way in a short time—and there’s much more to do. Get the information about who we are, where we’ve been, and where we’re headed.

Started in Calcutta , our company has helped 5 million customers all over India . With our motto Get Help on the Go , our struggle to provide all possible help to our fellow users continues. Don't be left behind , get on board and let's pledge to help each other whenever and wherever we can .

How it all started

On a rainy Calcutta evening in 2015, Manish Sharma and Priyanka Joshi had trouble with their car. So they came up with a simple idea—tap a button, get help.

What started as an app to request premium mechanic service in a few metropolitan areas is now changing the logistical fabric of cities around the world. Whether it’s a quick fix, an emergency , or taking their whole vehicle , we use technology to give people what they want, when and where they want it.

For the women and men who drive , our app represents a flexible new way to be safe and get help on the go . For cities, we help strengthen local economies, improve access to transportation, and make streets safer. When you make transportation as reliable as running water, everyone benefits. Especially when it’s raining outside.
